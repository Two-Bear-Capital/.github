## About

Two Bear Capital partners with founders building at the intersection of **life
sciences** and **technology** — disruptive sectors where breakthroughs can
scale. Our team of investors, operators, and advisors brings deep domain
expertise across both worlds, and we invest early to help build companies
designed for longevity.

## What We Invest In

| 🧬 Life Sciences | 💻 Technology |
|---|---|
| Therapeutics & drug discovery | Cybersecurity & post-quantum |
| Oncology & immunology | Infrastructure & networking |
| Health & bio platforms | Data, AI & developer tools |
